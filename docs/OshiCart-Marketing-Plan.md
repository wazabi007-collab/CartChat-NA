# OshiCart Marketing & Launch Plan

## Target Audience

| Segment | Description | Where to find them |
|---|---|---|
| WhatsApp Sellers | People selling via WhatsApp Status & DMs | WhatsApp groups, Facebook marketplace |
| Food Businesses | Kapana stands, takeaways, bakeries, caterers | Local food groups, markets |
| Fashion/Retail | Boutiques, thrift stores, clothing brands | Instagram, Facebook pages |
| Service Providers | Salons, mechanics, photographers, consultants | Local business directories |
| Artisans | Handmade goods, crafts, jewelry | Craft markets, Instagram |

---

## Marketing Channels

### 1. WhatsApp (Primary — this is where your market lives)
- **WhatsApp Status**: Post daily stories showing OshiCart features
- **WhatsApp Broadcast Lists**: Build lists of potential merchants
- **WhatsApp Groups**: Join/create business groups in Namibia
- **Forward-friendly content**: Create messages merchants will forward to each other

### 2. Facebook (Namibia's most-used social platform)
- **Facebook Page**: OshiCart official page
- **Facebook Groups**: Post in Namibian business groups (Buy & Sell Windhoek, Namibian Entrepreneurs, etc.)
- **Facebook Ads**: Targeted ads to small business owners in Namibia (as low as N$50/day)
- **Facebook Marketplace sellers**: DM sellers who are struggling with manual orders

### 3. Instagram
- **Reels**: Short product demo videos (15-30 seconds)
- **Stories**: Daily tips for online sellers
- **Carousel posts**: Feature comparisons, how-to guides
- **Hashtags**: #NamibianBusiness #SellOnline #OshiCart #MadeInNamibia #WhatsAppBusiness

### 4. TikTok
- **Short demos**: "Watch me set up a store in 2 minutes"
- **Before/after**: "How I went from WhatsApp DMs to a professional store"
- **Trends**: Use trending sounds with relatable merchant content
- **Target**: Young entrepreneurs, side-hustle culture

### 5. YouTube
- **Tutorials**: "How to set up your OshiCart store" (full walkthrough)
- **Customer stories**: Feature early merchants and their success
- **Feature demos**: Individual feature deep-dives

### 6. Local/Offline
- **Business networking events** in Windhoek
- **Market days** — demo OshiCart to vendors at craft/food markets
- **Radio ads** — Namibian radio stations (affordable, wide reach)
- **Flyers/QR codes** — at shopping centers, food courts
- **Partnerships** — banks (FNB, Standard Bank), MTC, business incubators
