About

What is OshiCart?

From DMs to a professional storefront — in minutes.

OshiCart is a proudly Namibian e-commerce platform that turns WhatsApp-based businesses into professional online stores. Merchants get a personalized store link (e.g., oshicart.com/s/your-store) where customers can browse products and services, place orders, and pay — all without downloading an app.

We know that in Namibia, business happens in the DMs and on WhatsApp Status. OshiCart bridges that gap — turning casual chats into a professional, automated storefront. We handle the tech; you secure the bag.
